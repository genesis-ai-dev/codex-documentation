Once you have created your Codex Editor account, you can log in to access all translation features and your projects. This guide covers the login process and session management.

## Login Process

<LoomVideo videoId="2069f6f2e88e462493e3c1a46a23cdb2" timestamp={84} title="Logging In" />

### Automatic Login After Registration
- After creating your account, you will be logged in automatically
- No additional login steps are required immediately after registration

### Manual Login
When you need to log in manually:

1. **Open Codex Editor**: Launch the application
2. **Enter Credentials**: 
   - Username or email address
   - Your account password
3. **Click Login**: Submit your credentials to access your account

## Session Management

### Session Duration
- You will remain logged in for up to **one year**
- This means you won't need to log in frequently
- Your session persists across application restarts

### Staying Logged In
- Codex Editor automatically saves your login state
- You can close and reopen the application without losing your session
- Projects and settings are preserved between sessions

## Security Features

### Automatic Logout
Your session may automatically expire if:
- You haven't used the application for an extended period
- There are security concerns detected
- You manually log out from another device

### Multiple Device Access
- You can be logged in on multiple devices simultaneously
- Projects sync across all logged-in devices
- Changes made on one device appear on others

## Troubleshooting Login Issues

### Forgot Password
If you can't remember your password:
1. Click **"Forgot Password"** on the login screen
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Follow the link to create a new password

### Account Locked
If your account appears locked:
- Wait a few minutes and try again
- Ensure you're using the correct credentials
- Contact support if the issue persists

### Login Not Working
Common solutions:
- Verify your internet connection
- Check that you're using the correct username/email
- Ensure Caps Lock is not accidentally enabled
- Try clearing the application cache

## Managing Your Session

### Logging Out
To manually log out:
1. Go to the main menu
2. Select **"Account Settings"** or **"Profile"**
3. Click **"Log Out"**

### Switching Accounts
If you need to use a different account:
1. Log out of your current account
2. Return to the login screen
3. Enter the credentials for the other account

## Security Best Practices

### Keep Your Account Secure
- Don't share your login credentials
- Log out when using shared computers
- Use a strong, unique password
- Keep your email account secure

### Regular Security Checks
- Review your account activity periodically
- Update your password if you suspect it's been compromised
- Ensure your email address is current and accessible
