## Loading the Codex App

1. **Launch the Application**: Open the Codex Editor application on your device.
2. **Accept Permissions**: When prompted, accept any necessary permissions that the application requests.
3. **Restart if Needed**: If the application doesn't load properly after accepting permissions, close and reopen it.

## Account Registration Process

Creating an account with Codex Editor gives you access to all translation features, project management tools, and collaboration capabilities.

### Step 1: Access the Registration Screen
1. From the initial screen, select the option to create a new account.

![Screenshot of the login page](/images/create-account.png)

### Step 2: Enter Your Information

<Callout type="info">
We recommend writing down your login details somewhere safe
</Callout>

1. **Username**:
   - Must be unique across all Codex Editor users
   - Can contain letters, numbers, and underscores
   - Cannot be changed after account creation

2. **Email Address**:
   <Callout type="warning">
   Use a real email address or a reliable burner email
   </Callout>
   - Fake emails cannot be used for password resets
   - You may receive important notifications at this address

3. **Password**:
   - Minimum length of 15 characters required
   - Must include at least one uppercase and one lowercase letter
   - Numbers and symbols are not required
   - Confirm your password by entering it again
   - Click the eye icon to view the password if needed

![Screenshot of the registration page](/images/registration-example.png)

4. **Register**
   - Click **Register** to complete your initial setup

### Automatic Login After Registration
- After creating your account, you will be logged in automatically
- You will remain logged in for up to one year
- This means you won't need to log in frequently
- Your session persists across application restarts

### Manual Login
When you need to log in manually:

1. **Open Codex Editor**: Launch the application
2. **Enter Credentials**: 
   - Username or email address (typically your email address will work best)
   - Your account password
3. **Click Login**: Submit your credentials to access your account

## Account Security Best Practices

### Password Guidelines

<Callout type="warning">
Passwords containing information from your username or email will be rejected
</Callout>

- Use a unique password that you don't use elsewhere
- Consider using a password manager
- Avoid personal information like birthdays or names

### Email Considerations
- Use an email you check regularly
- Ensure the email account is secure
- Add Codex Editor to your email whitelist to prevent important messages from going to spam

If you are having trouble installing Codex or logging in, work through the relevant section below before contacting support.

### The app will not open or is very slow to start

**Step 1 — Check your Codex version first**

Most "won't open" and slow-loading issues are caused by running an outdated version. Update before troubleshooting anything else.

1. Open Codex. Go to **Help > About Codex**.
2. Note the version number.
3. Compare it to the [latest release](/docs/releases/latest).
4. If you are not on the latest version, update now and try again before continuing.

**Step 2 — Force quit and restart**

- **Windows**: Press `Ctrl + Shift + Esc` to open Task Manager. Find Codex in the list. Right-click > End Task. Reopen Codex.
- **Mac**: Press `Cmd + Option + Esc`. Select Codex. Click Force Quit. Reopen Codex.
- **Linux**: Run `killall codex` in a terminal. Reopen Codex.

**Step 3 — Restart your computer**

A full reboot clears memory and temporary files that a force quit does not. This resolves many loading issues.

**Step 4 — Check your internet connection**

Codex requires internet access for AI features and project sync. Open a browser and confirm you can load a website. If your connection is unstable, resolve that first.

**Step 5 — Check antivirus or firewall (Windows)**

Antivirus software can block Codex from starting. Temporarily disable your antivirus and try again. If Codex opens, add it to your antivirus exceptions list.

Codex requires access to these domains:

- `api.frontierrnd.com`
- `gitlab.com` and `git.door43.org`
- `github.com` and `api.github.com`
- `raw.githubusercontent.com`
- `registry.npmjs.org`
- `lff.api.languagetechnology.org`

**Step 6 — macOS permissions (Gatekeeper)**

1. Go to **System Preferences > Security & Privacy**.
2. If you see a message saying Codex was blocked, click **Open Anyway**.
3. You may need to enter your Mac password to confirm.

### Login and account issues

#### The "username" field requires your email address

<Callout type="warning">
The login field is labelled **Username** but it requires your **email address**. If you are getting an "invalid credentials" error, try entering your email address instead of your username. This is a known labelling issue — using your email address resolves it in almost all cases.
</Callout>

#### Cannot log in after registering

After registering, Codex logs you in automatically. You should not need to log in again immediately. If you are prompted to log in:

1. Enter the **email address** you used to register (not your username).
2. Enter your password.
3. If you see an error message, note the exact text. Complete the [Troubleshooting guide](/docs/troubleshooting) support form and paste the generated template into [Discord](https://discord.gg/6kVJTEXYEp).
4. If you forgot your password, use the **Forgot Password** option on the login screen. Check your spam folder for the reset email.

#### Registration is not working

Make sure your password meets all three requirements:

- At least **15 characters** long
- At least one **uppercase** letter
- At least one **lowercase** letter

<Callout type="info">
Numbers and symbols are not required. If the password field is showing an error, check that your password is at least 15 characters and contains both uppercase and lowercase letters.
</Callout>

If registration still fails, complete the [Troubleshooting guide](/docs/troubleshooting) support form with the exact error message, then paste the generated template into [Discord](https://discord.gg/6kVJTEXYEp).

#### Common account issues

- **Username already taken**: Try variations of your preferred username, or add numbers or underscores to make it unique.
- **Email already registered**: You may already have an account. Try the password reset option if you have forgotten your credentials.
