# Initial Setup

After you install Codex, launch the desktop app from your applications menu or desktop shortcut. The first launch may take longer than later launches because Codex checks for required extensions and installs any missing core extensions from Open VSX.

<Callout type="info">
If you are setting up Codex for the first time, stay online until the startup screen appears. Later editing can continue offline for local projects, but first launch, login, project download, sync, AI, and new extension pins need network access.
</Callout>

## First Launch Checklist

1. Open **Codex**.
2. Approve operating-system prompts, such as opening an unsigned app or allowing microphone access for audio workflows.
3. Wait for the startup screen to finish loading.
4. If Codex asks to reload after extension setup, allow the reload.
5. Sign in or create an account.

Codex includes built-in app components that install and manage the translation extensions. If a team project requires specific extension versions, Codex may switch into a pinned profile and reload before the project opens.

## Create an Account

1. On the startup screen, choose the option to create a new account.
2. Enter a username, email address, and password.
3. Click **Register**.
4. After registration, Codex signs you in automatically.

![Screenshot of the registration page](/images/registration-example.png)

### Username

- Usernames must be unique.
- Usernames are case-sensitive.
- You can also use your email address when logging in.
- Usernames cannot be changed after account creation.

### Email Address

Use an email address you can access. Password resets are sent by email, so fake or inaccessible addresses can lock you out of your account.

### Password

Passwords must:

- Be at least **15 characters** long.
- Avoid using parts of your username or email address.

<Callout type="warning">
The current visible validation is length plus avoiding username/email fragments. If Codex shows a stricter message in the app, follow the app message.
</Callout>

## Sign In Later

When you need to sign in manually:

1. Open Codex.
2. Enter your username or email address.
3. Enter your password.
4. Click **Login**.

Your session normally persists across app restarts. You may be asked to sign in again after a token expires, after clearing app data, or when opening a project that needs cloud access.

<Callout type="info" title="Forgot your password?">
Use **Forgot Password** on the login screen. See [Reset Forgotten Password](/docs/getting-started/forgot-password).
</Callout>

<Accordion title="Troubleshooting setup and login">

### Codex will not open or is very slow to start

1. Force quit Codex and reopen it.
2. Restart your computer if force quit does not help.
3. Confirm your internet connection works.
4. Check antivirus or firewall settings, especially on Windows. See [Antivirus Blockages](/docs/antivirus-blockages).
5. Check your app version in **Help > About Codex** and compare it with [Release Notes](/docs/releases/latest).

### Core extensions are missing or Codex keeps reloading

Codex may be applying extension version pins for a project. Stay online and let the reload finish. If Codex stops with an error report, include that report when asking for support.

### Registration is not working

Check that your password is at least 15 characters and does not include parts of your username or email address. If registration still fails, copy the exact error message and use the [Troubleshooting guide](/docs/troubleshooting).

### Login says invalid credentials

Usernames are case-sensitive. Try your email address instead of your username. If you cannot remember the password, use [Forgot Password](/docs/getting-started/forgot-password).
