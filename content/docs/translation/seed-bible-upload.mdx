---
title: Uploading to Seed Bible
description: Use the Codex Seed Bible Upload extension to upload your translation to the Seed Bible project and share it with a link
---

The **Codex Seed Bible Upload** extension lets you upload your translation to the Seed Bible project directly from Codex. After uploading, you get a link you can copy and share with others.

The extension is not installed by default — you'll need to install it from the Extensions panel first.

## Step 1: Install the Extension

1. Click the **Extensions** icon <img src="/images/extensions-icon.svg" alt="Extensions icon" style={{width: '16px', height: '16px', display: 'inline', verticalAlign: 'middle'}} /> in the sidebar (or press `Ctrl + Shift + X` / `Cmd + Shift + X`)
2. Search for **`aolab.codex-seed-bible-upload`**
3. Look for **Codex Seed Bible Upload** by **AO Lab** and click **Install**

![Codex Seed Bible Upload extension by AO Lab](/images/seed-bible-extension.png)

## Step 2: Log In

Once installed, open the Seed Bible panel and go to the **Account** tab. Click **Log In** and enter your email address — you'll receive a code by email to complete the login.

![Seed Bible panel Account tab with the Log In button](/images/seed-bible-login.png)

<Callout type="info">
Your email is not stored by Seed Bible. It's only used to send you the one-time code, as a way to verify the request is coming from a person rather than a bot.
</Callout>

## Step 3: Fill Out the Metadata

Switch to the **Metadata** tab and fill out the information about your translation.

<Callout type="warn">
Metadata is saved to the project workspace on your computer, but it does **not** sync between users. If a teammate uploads from their own machine, they will need to fill out the metadata themselves.
</Callout>

## Step 4: Upload

Go to the **Upload** tab and click **Upload**. When the upload finishes, you'll get a link that you can copy and share with anyone you want to show the translation to.
