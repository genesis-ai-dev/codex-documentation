## Using AI to Generate Translation Instructions

### 1. Introduction to AI Instructions

<LoomVideo videoId="478cd35149d34f5095eba83319b01737" timestamp={0} title="Introduction to AI Instructions" />

- Brief overview of the purpose: 
  - To show how AI can generate instructions for better predictions.
  - Focus on improving translation processes.

### 2. Accessing Project Management Tab

<LoomVideo videoId="478cd35149d34f5095eba83319b01737" timestamp={13} title="Accessing Project Management Tab" />

- Navigate to the project management tab.
- Select a source language and a target language.

### 3. Opening Co-Pilot Settings

<LoomVideo videoId="478cd35149d34f5095eba83319b01737" timestamp={24} title="Opening Co-Pilot Settings" />

- Click on the co-pilot settings button to access workspace settings.

### 4. Updating Settings

<LoomVideo videoId="478cd35149d34f5095eba83319b01737" timestamp={35} title="Updating Settings" />

- Options to write instructions manually or use the sparkle button for AI assistance.
- Click the sparkle button to generate instructions.

### 5. Generating Translation Instructions

<LoomVideo videoId="478cd35149d34f5095eba83319b01737" timestamp={44} title="Generating Translation Instructions" />

- AI generates instructions for translators regarding language differences.
- Emphasize that the generated instructions serve as a starting point.

### 6. Customizing Instructions

<LoomVideo videoId="478cd35149d34f5095eba83319b01737" timestamp={65} title="Customizing Instructions" />

- Translators can modify the AI-generated instructions for specificity.

### 7. Saving and Reviewing Settings

<LoomVideo videoId="478cd35149d34f5095eba83319b01737" timestamp={79} title="Saving and Reviewing Settings" />

- Press save to update the settings.
- Check the updated settings to ensure changes are applied.

### 8. Conclusion

<LoomVideo videoId="478cd35149d34f5095eba83319b01737" timestamp={96} title="Conclusion" />

- Highlight the ease of updating instructions for LLMs (Language Learning Models).
